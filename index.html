Frequently asked questions

Why use Cuisn?

Establish and grow a self-owned food servicing business. Discover and order high end meals. Access diverse recipies.

As a chef how does Cuisn help me do that?

We leverage the power of social communites, and the love for food, to help foodies find you. We also automate the entire process of getting packaging, and organizing quality deliveries.

But that is only half the problem. The main challenge has always been capital. How does Cuisn solve for that?

Finances is the major problem chefs face in establishing a self-owned food servicing business. Cuisn uses a pre-order system as opposed to selling on-demand food. This means that no personal capital from the chef is involved.

Will I be able to order from chefs I don't follow??

Yes, access to diverse options is the core of Cuisn. You can easily find current available menus in the menu section and place an order.

Can Cuisn help me learn to cook meals as well?

Yes, there's an extensive "learn to cook" feature that you can take advantage of. It includes videos and delivery of ingredients. You can also find free cooking tips and diverse recipes posted by chefs.

What is the assurance

Chefs on Cuisn are vetted and verified by a team of talented and experienced in-house chef consultants. We are committed to providing the best quality high-end chefs and meals.
